# Amazon Sales Analytics Dashboard

An end-to-end data visualization solution designed to analyze Amazon sales performance through dynamic KPIs, trends, product performance, and customer demand patterns across regions and categories.

1. Purpose / Overview

The Amazon Sales Dashboard is developed to help business stakeholders monitor overall sales growth, identify top-performing products, evaluate regional demand, and track sales patterns across months and weeks.
The dashboard simplifies raw sales data into meaningful insights to support business strategy, marketing decisions, and inventory planning.

4.Features / Dashboard Highlights
> Business Problem

Amazon sellers and business analysts face difficulty in tracking sales performance, identifying profitable products, recognizing regional growth opportunities, and understanding seasonal customer trends from raw data.

> Goal of the Dashboard

To provide a unified analytical view that:

Shows the sales performance at product, time, and regional level

Helps decision-makers identify top revenue channels and trends

Supports inventory forecasting, pricing strategy & customer insights

5.Business Impact & Insights

  ~ Inventory Optimization: Helps forecast demand and stock planning based on top-selling categories and seasonal demand.

  ~ Marketing Strategy: Identifies which regions and categories need promotional focus.

  ~ Profitability Insights: Shows where the highest revenue comes from (category & product level).

  ~ Operational Efficiency: Tracks order status and fulfillment type to identify delivery or processing gaps.

  
  ~ Decision Making Speed: Converts raw, complex sales data into simplified & actionable insights for sellers and management.
